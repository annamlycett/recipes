Yellow rice:
*makes enough rice for 2 people*

Ingredients:
- 1 c organic white basmati rice
- a generous pat organic ghee or butter
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 4 cardamom pods
- 4 cloves
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tbsp fennel seeds
- Pink Himalayan salt, to taste

Utensils:
- lidded pot
- a spoon
- a measuring cup
- a kettle or a pan for boiling water

Put at least 3 cupfuls of water in the kettle/pan and start boiling it.
Put the ghee or butter in the pan and let it melt on a low heat.
Add the rice and stir it through. Add the turmeric, cardamom, cloves,
bay leaf, fennel seeds and salt. Stir through.
Pour 1.5 cups boiling water and stir through. Bring to a boil, then
reduce heat to a low simmer and cover with lid. Simmer for 12 minutes
without removing the lid to keep the steam in.
After 12 minutes switch the heat off and let stand for a few minutes
before serving with curry or any other well suited dish.
